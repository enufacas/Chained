**What it is:** A standard for building composable AI tools, similar to how REST standardized APIs.

**Why it matters:**
- Enables modular AI pipelines
- Reduces tool coupling
- Facilitates tool interoperability
- TrendRadar is an early adopter

**Prediction:** Will become mainstream in 6-12 months

**What it is:** Tools that monitor 30+ data sources simultaneously to solve information overload.

- Social media sentiment trackers

**Use cases:**
- Trend detection
- Market intelligence

- News curation
